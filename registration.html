IEEE RTCSA 2022

(Co-located with NVMSA 2022)

The 28th IEEE International Conference on Embedded and Real-Time Computing Systems and Applications


August 23-25, 2022, National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan

Conference Registration


The link to the registration website: https://cvent.me/DWDgyO.

Important note:

  • At least one author per paper must be registered as an AUTHOR at the full Member/Non-Member registration rate, regardless of whether or not they are students.
  • The author registration deadline is 20 August, 2022.
  • Each Author Registration is valid for ONE paper.
  • Each Author registration is non-refundable and is used to support exactly one conference paper.
  • Additional papers can be purchased per author registration for an additional fee.
  • Each paper may contain up to TWO pages for a fee of US$100 per page.
  • The Paper ID# can be located on the author submission site found in the easychair RTCSA submission site: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=rtcsa2022
  • After August 20, NON-AUTHOR registration can be done on-site.
  • For assitance in registration matters, please contact RTCSA 2022 Registration Chair Chao Wang at cw@ntnu.edu.tw.

    • The information above can also be found in the registration site.
