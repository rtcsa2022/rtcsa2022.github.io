Venue

IEEE RTCSA 2022 will be organized in the CSIE department buidling: 德田館 -- De Tian building. Click here to see its position in google map. From the front it looks like this:


If you enter through the front door, you will immediately see the reception desks and the rooms where RTCSA and NVMSA 2022 will be held.

The two nearest MRT station to CSIE department are:
  • Gongguan MRT station: about 15 minute walk according to google map.
  • Technology building MRT station: about 12 minute walk according to google map.

 


Keynote speeches

We are pleased that three distinguished computer scientists have agreed to give the keynote speeches in RTCSA 2022:
  • Tei-Wei Kuo (National Taiwan University).
  • K.S. Pua (Phison Electronics Corp.).
  • David Hsu (ARM Inc.).
For more details about them and their talks, see keynote.html.
